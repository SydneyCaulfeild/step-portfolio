My Projects

Learn about some of my recent projects.

2020 - Robot Racing

I collaborated on a team of four in my Mechatronics course to control a small motorized robot. We had to do all wire configurations and write code for the robot to find a paper target in a room as fast as possible. We used controlled the robot using Arduino and relied on IR sensors and the robot's bumpers to find the target.

2020 - WiSE Director of Web Design

I am this year's Director of Web Design with Women in Science and Engineering at Queen's University. I am responsible for designing the website, advertising the various programs and running online ticket sales for all events.

2019 - Vehicular Hyperthermia Prevention Project

I worked on a team of four in my Engineering Design course to design and test a system that would detect if a child or pet is forgotten in your car to prevent vehicular hyperthermia. Our final design included a combination of motion cameras, pressure sensors and a microphone that were controlled with a Wi-Fi enabled Arduino. The system sent an alert to the car owner's phone if it detected a child or pet.

2019 - EngLinks Tutor

I work as a one-on-one and group tutor and teach subjects such as Chemistry, Intro to Computer Programming and Lienar Algebra.
